# Gamma.app Presentation Prompt for SubDetox

Copy and paste the following prompt into Gamma.app (https://gamma.app/) to generate your 10-12 slide hackathon presentation.

***

**Prompt for Gamma.app:**

Please create a 12-slide hackathon pitch presentation for a project called "SubDetox: AI-Powered Subscription & Autopay Detox Agent". The target audience includes hackathon judges, technical experts, and business leaders. The tone should be professional, urgent, impactful, and technologically sophisticated. Use clean, modern layouts with high-contrast visuals suitable for a fintech product.

Here is the content and structure for the slides:

**Slide 1: Title & Hook**
*   **Title:** SubDetox
*   **Subtitle:** The AI-Powered Subscription & Autopay Detox Agent
*   **Hook:** Stop the silent wealth drain. Take back control of your money with one click.
*   **Visual:** A modern fintech aesthetic showing a shield protecting a wallet from multiple small, draining pipes.

**Slide 2: The Problem - The Silent Wealth Drain**
*   **Headline:** The Silent Wealth Drain
*   **Content:** Indian consumers lose thousands of rupees annually to fragmented, forgotten micro-subscriptions, hidden telecom VAS, and dormant UPI AutoPay mandates.
*   **Key Pain Points:** Scattered across multiple banking apps; users lack visibility; dark patterns make cancellation complex.

**Slide 3: Evidence & Context - A Growing Crisis**
*   **Headline:** A Billion-Transaction Crisis
*   **Data Points:** UPI AutoPay handles nearly 1 billion recurring debits monthly. Over 870 million active mandates on UPI (RBI data).
*   **The Issue:** A sharp surge in consumer complaints about unexplained involuntary debits. Manual auditing is practically impossible for the common man.

**Slide 4: The Solution - Absolute Control**
*   **Headline:** SubDetox: Your One-Click Control Center
*   **Content:** An AI-driven financial auditor that bypasses intentional complexity. It gives consumers absolute control over their recurring payments.
*   **Core Value:** Shift the power dynamic from corporations back to the consumer, reducing cognitive load to zero.

**Slide 5: How It Works (Step 1) - Consent-Based Discovery**
*   **Headline:** Step 1: Secure Data Ingestion
*   **Process:** Integrates with India’s Account Aggregator (AA) framework.
*   **Action:** A single OTP grants secure, read-only access to transaction histories across banks, credit cards, and UPI wallets, creating a single source of truth.

**Slide 6: How It Works (Step 2) - Agentic Pattern Recognition**
*   **Headline:** Step 2: AI-Powered Analysis
*   **Process:** Goes beyond official mandates. The AI agent parses raw transaction narratives.
*   **Action:** Identifies hidden recurring patterns, obscure VAS, and "zombie" subscriptions. Classifies debits by utility, frequency, and threat level (cost).

**Slide 7: How It Works (Step 3) - One-Click Revocation**
*   **Headline:** Step 3: Instant Action
*   **Process:** A brutalist, stripped-down dashboard shows exactly where money leaks.
*   **Action:** A single "Revoke" button triggers bank APIs to kill UPI/e-NACH mandates, or generates legally binding cancellation emails/deep-links for card subscriptions.

**Slide 8: Architecture & Tech Stack**
*   **Headline:** Pragmatic, Scalable Architecture
*   **Frontend:** Flutter mobile app (cross-platform, stateful dashboard).
*   **Backend:** FastAPI (Python) for AA-style orchestration and analysis APIs.
*   **Identity/Data:** Firebase Auth & Firestore.
*   **AI Engine:** Deterministic rules engine with optional Gemini LLM enrichment (ensuring high availability even if AI fails).
*   **Deployment:** Cloud Run.

**Slide 9: Target Audience**
*   **Headline:** Who Benefits?
*   **Primary Audience:** Urban/semi-urban middle class (Gen Z & Millennials) suffering from subscription fatigue.
*   **Secondary Audience:** Older demographics (Gen X & Boomers) vulnerable to deceptive auto-renewals and lacking digital literacy to navigate complex banking apps.

**Slide 10: Quantifiable Impact**
*   **Headline:** Real-World Value Creation
*   **Financial Salvage:** Reclaims ₹1,500 to ₹3,000 annually per user.
*   **Time Saved:** Compresses 10-15 minute cancellation loops into a 5-second, single-click action.
*   **Macro Potential:** Capturing just 1 million users prevents ₹150+ crores in involuntary wealth drain annually.

**Slide 11: Future Scope & Roadmap**
*   **Headline:** The Future of SubDetox
*   **Short-term:** Direct integration with more bank APIs for instant mandate cancellation; enhanced localized NLP for obscure regional bank narrations.
*   **Long-term:** Expansion into proactive budget planning, personalized subscription negotiation, and integration with credit scoring models.

**Slide 12: Conclusion & Q&A**
*   **Headline:** Reclaiming Consumer Power
*   **Summary:** SubDetox is not just an app; it is a financial consumer protection tool built for the modern digital economy.
*   **Call to Action:** Join us in stopping the silent wealth drain.
*   **Footer:** Q&A / Thank You.
